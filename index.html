My Studio

Ars Longa Piano Studio, provides inspiring, personalized piano instruction for beginners to advanced musicians meeting you where you are at and taking you to the next level in your musical journey. This happens in a safe, creative, and engaging environment, blending classical techniques with creative expression, to build confidence, artistry and a life long love of music.

Teaching Philosophy

  • Create a safe and positive environment that accommodates the needs of diverse learners.
  • Foster and promote student growth through their own personal voice
  • Continuously re-examining teaching practices to align with best evidence based practices and research.

    • Instructor

    Dylan Griffith is a classically trained, multi-instrumentalist, composer, performer, music educator. With over 20 years of musical experience, Dylan has exercised her commitment to the arts, serving as a music professor and the musical director for the C.A.M.A program at Tennessee State University. She currently serves as an assistant professor of music and collaborative pianist at Lane College. She obtained her B.A at Spelman College, her M.M in music education at Belmont University, and her M.M in Piano Performance at the University of Memphis. She is currently working on her D.M.A in Piano performance at the University of Memphis.

    She has taught a variety of courses ranging from piano courses, history courses, beginner and advanced theory.
